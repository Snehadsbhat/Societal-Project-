About Our School

Welcome to Our School

A good education system in synthesis with a rich cultural heritage forms the nucleus of a proactive and humane civil society and only such socitey can be the foundation of gratest nation. It is only in such an environment, where valued education is available to the poorest of the poor, shall a civil society see human welfare and real development in the true human spirit.

It is in this direction and with blessings and guidance of Paramapoojya Sri sri Raghaveshwara Bharathi Swamy of Sri Ramachandrapura Mutt that our seva trust decided to open the Sri Prajnabharathi Vidya Kendra in Nittur, Hosanagara Taulk, Shimoga District.

Prajnabharathi Vidya Kendra is cradled in the heavenly greens of the western ghats. It is situated in the valley of Kodachadri the holy land of Sri Shankaracharya, which is very close to Kodachadri-Kollur road and in the vicinity of the famous Sri Kollur Mookambika.

Our Vision

At present, We have a school that imparts primary and high school education. Since the surronding population is mostly poor and middle class and cannot afford primary education. Our trust has been imparting valued education without the burden of higher donation to its students.

Nalanda Seva Trust also has a plan for number of social welfare programmes such as rural welfare, health and medical care, environmental awareness, development of local crafts, protection of medicinal plants, library, physical education, theater work shops and painting.

Our Mission

  • We have an ambitious project in our mind to upgrade the english medium higher school and residential school facilities.
  • We also have decided to impart personality development programmes with special emphasis on our culture and heritage, which involves sanskrit,yoga,music,arts,etc which will strengthen the character of every student.
  • We also have plans to construct a 'Samudaya Bhavana' that will suit the requirement of the local people as it serves as an auditorium to the school as well.
