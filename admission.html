Admissions - Prajnabharathi Vidya Kendra

Welcome to Admissions

At Prajnabharathi Vidya Kendra, we are committed to providing quality education in a nurturing environment. Admissions are open for the academic year, and we invite parents and guardians to explore our holistic approach to education.

Admission Process:

  • Fill out the admission form with accurate details.
  • Submit the following documents along with the form:
    • Birth Certificate
    • Previous Academic Records (if applicable)
    • Passport-sized photographs

We encourage parents to get involved in their child's educational journey and provide necessary support at home. Let us work together to shape a bright future for your child!
