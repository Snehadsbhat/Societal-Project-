Academic Achievements

Our students represent the school at the district level every year in Pratibha Karanji competitions.
In the 10th-grade annual exams, the school consistently achieves a 100% pass rate, with the highest recorded score of 623 out of 625.
Every year, 5 to 6 students score more than 600 marks in the final exams.
In 2016 and 2017, students represented the school at the state level in the INSPIRE Awards.
Every year, a school annual day is organized with a dedicated theme.
3 to 4 students receive free education annually.

Sports Achievements

Our students consistently represent the school in chess at the district level.
In the academic year 2019–20, six students represented the school in chess at the district level.
Swagath Bhat of Grade 6 represented the school in chess at the district level in the current academic year.
Nishchay from Grade 10 participated at the state level in shot put and secured 6th place.
Abhijna M.M from Grade 7 represented the school in yoga at the district level.

Extra Curricular Achievements

A 10 km cleanliness and plastic eradication campaign was successfully conducted from Nittur to Sampekatte.
Students participated in various exhibitions like history, science, literature, folk festivals, and food festivals.
Aparna B.B of Grade 10 participated in the Karnataka State Literary Conference in 2019–20, and was elected as Taluk Literary Council President in 2018–19.
Activities such as handwritten book creation, literary events, field visits, motivational classes, and summer camps deeply inspired students.
Street plays focused on environmental awareness are conducted every year during Independence Day and Kannada Rajyotsava.
